Introduction

Pump Doge (PDOGE) is a professionally designed memecoin launched on the pump.fun platform, powered by the Solana blockchain. Created to unite the community, bring excitement, and offer the potential for profits. PDOGE celebrates the humor of memecoins with a modern, professional touch.

Key Details

  • Blockchain: Solana
  • Platform: pump.fun
  • Total Supply: 140,000,000,000 PDOGE (100% unlocked at launch)
  • Purpose: For fun, community, pumps, and profits

Philosophy

  • Community-driven: The future of Pump Doge is decided entirely by its community.
  • For the pump: PDOGE aims to create excitement and reward its holders.
  • For fun: Humor and a sense of community are central to PDOGE's values.

NFT Collection & DAO

100 days after launch on pump.fun, a unique collection of 140 Pump Doge NFTs will be airdropped. These NFTs will:

  • Grant membership to the exclusive PDOGE DAO Lodge, limited to 140 members.
  • Be tradable on the Solana blockchain.
  • Each NFT holds equal voting power.
  • Multiple NFTs in one wallet count as one vote during governance decisions.
